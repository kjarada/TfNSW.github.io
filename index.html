Providing accurate surveying & spatial data for NSW transport infrastructure projects.

About Us

The Transport for NSW (TfNSW) Surveying Team plays a crucial role in planning and delivering the state’s transport infrastructure. Our surveyors provide accurate spatial data, mapping, and land surveying services to ensure that projects – from roads and bridges to rail lines – are designed and built with precision and efficiency.

Using state-of-the-art equipment and techniques, we support engineers and planners across New South Wales with reliable measurements and data. We are committed to safety, accuracy, and innovation in every project we undertake.

  • Conducting land and engineering surveys for road, rail, and infrastructure projects.
  • Providing spatial data analysis, digital mapping, and geospatial insights for planning and design.
  • Ensuring construction aligns with design specifications through precise on-site measurements.
